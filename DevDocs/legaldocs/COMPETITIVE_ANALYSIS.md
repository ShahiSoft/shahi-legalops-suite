## üìä EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The Legal Documents Generator market is dominated by subscription-based SaaS platforms (Termly, Iubenda) and simpler template-based WordPress plugins (CookieYes, Complianz). However, there are significant gaps that our integrated, AI-powered module can exploit:

1. **No true AI integration** - Competitors use static templates, not intelligent content generation
2. **Isolated solutions** - Each plugin works independently; ours integrates with Accessibility Scanner, Consent Manager, DSR Portal
3. **Limited customization** - Code editor access rare; most limit users to templates
4. **Manual compliance tracking** - Regulations change are handled manually; our module auto-tracks
5. **Poor legal team workflows** - No built-in approval/review processes
6. **Limited document types** - Most offer 3-5 documents; we'll offer 12+
7. **WooCommerce gap** - Weak eCommerce integration; we have deep WooCommerce awareness
8. **Developer accessibility** - Most have limited or no API; we offer full REST API + WP-CLI
